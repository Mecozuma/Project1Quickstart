Poker 414 Rules

  • After shuffling the deck, the dealer serves all players two cards, heads down.
  • Players bet on whether their hand is the best.
  • The order in which players have to act moves clockwise around the table.
  • There are usually two or more betting intervals for each poker deal.
  • After the final interval, there is a "showdown" where each player shows their hand face up on the table.
  • The best poker hand takes the pot.
  • If a player makes a bet or raise that no other player calls, they win the pot without showing their hand.
